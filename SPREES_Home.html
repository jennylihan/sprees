We are a community of undergraduates who are passionate about education.


The Stanford Pre-Education Society (SPREES) is Stanford University's first pre-professional society for students pursuing careers in education. We believe in attracting talented college graduates to the field of education and providing them with the support they need to get there. Though students at "elite" institutions like Stanford often overlook careers in teaching and education, SPREES believes that guidance and mentorship can inspire exceptional students to become the teachers and leaders we need in the field.
